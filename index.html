Geotropism Stimulator

Drag the magnifier onto a tip, then tilt the pot to see auxin redistribute and the tip curl.

t = 24h

Shoot

Root

Muted color = growth that already existed when the pot was tilted (rigid, doesn't re-bend). Bright color = growth added since then, which steers back toward vertical over time — faster in the root than the shoot in this model. Bending is concentrated near the tip, in the elongation zone, as it is in real plants.
